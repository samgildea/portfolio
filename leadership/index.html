My Advice

Reach Out For Help

While studying computer science at Northeastern, it can be very challenging at times. However, there are many other students struggling with the same issues and there a many different places you can reach out for help that are offered in CCIS.

Explore Northeastern For Things That Interest You

Throughout my first semester, I was very surprised with the amount of clubs and people that all share interests as me. Having groups of people that all share the same interests as me made my first semester much less stressful and enjoyable.

Discover What's Important To You And Cut Out Time For Those Things

Throughout my first semester, one thing that really helped make my time more enjoyable was discovering what truly interestd me and focusing on pursuing those interests. Everyone has different interests and what is interesting to one person isn't always interesting for you.
